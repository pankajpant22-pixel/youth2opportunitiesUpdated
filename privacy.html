Who we are

Y2O Growth Inc is a Canadian company building a single, student-owned record for what high school students do beyond grades. The product launches first across the GTA.

What we collect on this website

On youth2opportunities.ca we collect what you choose to share when you join the waitlist or send a message — name, email address, role, grade, city or school board, and any optional note.

We also collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) to keep the site running and to understand how it is used.

Why we collect it

To respond to messages, send waitlist updates, and improve the website. We do not use this information for advertising and we do not build advertising profiles.

Student ownership

Once the Y2O product is live, students own their profile. The student decides what to log, what to share, and who to share it with. Parents and partners see only what the student chooses to share with them, when the student chooses to share it.

Youth and minors

Y2O is built for high school students. Where required by law, we ask for parent or guardian consent. We collect the minimum information needed to operate the product and we never sell information about minors.

Tools we use

We use trusted third-party services for hosting, email, analytics, and waitlist management. Those services have their own privacy practices, which we review before adopting them.

No data selling

We do not sell student, family, or partner information to advertisers, brokers, or any third party. Verification data is shared only with the people a student chooses to share it with.

Your rights

You can ask what we hold about you, ask us to update it, or ask us to delete it. Email privacy@youth2opportunities.ca and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product develops and as laws change. The current version is always posted on this page, with the update date noted below.

Contact

For privacy questions, write to privacy@youth2opportunities.ca. For general questions, write to hello@youth2opportunities.ca.
