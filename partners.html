How it works

From request to confirmation in under a minute

A simple flow with no extra software, no login, and no paper.

01

Student logs the activity

They enter what happened, where it happened, and how long it took.

02

You get a short request

The request arrives by email, SMS, or another supported channel.

03

You confirm or decline

One tap confirms what you saw. Declining marks it as self-reported.

04

The record updates

The student’s activity record updates automatically.

What verification means

  • You confirm only what the student says you witnessed.
  • You do not see grades, private profile details, or unrelated activity.
  • If something is wrong, you can decline.

    Your name stays connected to the confirmed activity for trust and accountability.
