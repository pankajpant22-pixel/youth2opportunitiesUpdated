What we are building

One record for the work students actually do

Grades show one part of a student. Y2O shows the verified effort behind the student.

  • Activities and hours, logged as they happen.
  • Skills shown in the context where they were built.
  • Named confirmations from the people who saw the work.

The origin

The question we could not answer clearly

Varya was doing what many students do: tutoring, working, helping at home, joining teams, and building real skills. The problem was simple. Most of that work did not appear on a transcript, résumé, or application in a trusted way.

The honest answer was nowhere. So we started building Y2O: a record students can own, update, verify, and carry forward.

What we believe

Five values we will not compromise

Every product decision starts here. If a choice does not pass these values, it does not ship.

01

Student-owned

The record belongs to the student, across schools, applications, and life after graduation.

02

Verified by people

Confirmation by a real person beats an unverified claim or black-box algorithm.

03

Built with students

Students test the product. If they would not use it, we redesign it.

04

Built with partners

Schools, employers, programs, and communities shape what verification looks like in practice.

05

Built for what comes next

Y2O must work beyond Grade 12 for scholarships, jobs, admissions, and early talent pathways.
