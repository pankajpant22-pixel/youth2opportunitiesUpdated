Why we're reaching out

Students show up. The system doesn't see it.

Every year, students across the GTA put in real hours at organizations like yours. They volunteer at food banks. They learn robotics at after-school programs. They work weekends at restaurants. They compete on sports teams. They lead projects at community centres.

But when they apply for university, scholarships, or their first real job — none of it is on the record. Their transcript shows grades. It doesn't show the hundreds of hours they spent growing with you.

It doesn't matter whether those hours were spent stacking shelves at a food bank, debugging code at a robotics club, serving customers at a cafe, or training for a tournament. The system treats them all the same way: invisible.

Ontario just introduced the Minister's Certificate of Recognition — Bronze, Silver, and Gold tiers for students who go beyond 40 community involvement hours. It goes on their official record. But the infrastructure to track and verify those hours doesn't exist. Neither does any system for tracking work experience, learning hours, or competitive participation.

That's why we're building Y2O.

How it works

You don't have to change anything about how you work.

No new software. No training. No admin. No cost.

1

A student adds your organization

When a student logs an activity on Y2O — volunteering, a class, a shift, a game — they tag your organization. You don't need to be on the platform.

2

You receive a link

By email, text, or WhatsApp — your choice. The link shows the student's name, what they did, and the hours they're claiming. No login required.

3

You tap to confirm

"Yes, this student participated." That's it. Their record is now verified. 10 seconds. You've just helped a student's growth become visible.

Works the same whether you're a temple, a tutoring centre, a restaurant, or a sports league.

What is Y2O?

Y2O — Youth to Opportunities — is the verified growth record for youth. We help high school students capture everything they build outside the classroom — volunteering, learning, working, competing, leading — in one profile, verified by the organizations and employers they work with.

Founded by a father and daughter in Halton Region who asked a simple question: why does the world want proof of who you are before giving you the chance to show it?

We're launching in the GTA. 350,000+ high school students. Your community.
